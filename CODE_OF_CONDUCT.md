# Code of Conduct

DAA Microbench is a research and engineering project. We want discussion to be rigorous, direct, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of people and precise about ideas.
- Give constructive technical feedback with enough context to act on it.
- Assume good faith, but correct errors clearly.
- Credit prior work, papers, tools, datasets, and contributors.
- Keep benchmark claims reproducible and evidence-based.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or repeated hostile comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately misleading benchmark claims.
- Attempts to bypass benchmark fairness rules in submitted results.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the project maintainers through the private reporting channel listed in the repository security/contact information once the public repository is created.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject submissions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.
